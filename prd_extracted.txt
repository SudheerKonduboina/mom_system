PRD – AI Meeting Intelligence System
1. Product Vision
The system is an AI-powered meeting assistant that automatically captures live meetings from Google Meet, transcribes multilingual conversations, identifies speakers, extracts meeting insights, and generates structured Minutes of Meeting (MOM) in real time.
The system eliminates manual note-taking and produces structured meeting intelligence automatically.
2. Core Capabilities
2.1 Live Streaming Transcription
Goal
Transcribe the meeting while it is happening, not after the meeting ends.
Functional Requirements
The system must:
Capture live audio from the meeting tab
Stream audio chunks to the backend
Transcribe speech continuously
Update transcript every few seconds
Support bilingual speech
Supported languages:
Hindi
English
Hindi-English mixed (Hinglish)
Implementation Architecture
Audio streaming pipeline:
Google Meet
     ↓
Chrome Tab Audio Capture
     ↓
Offscreen Recorder
     ↓
Audio Chunks (3–5 sec)
     ↓
WebSocket / Streaming API
     ↓
Backend AI Pipeline
     ↓
Live Transcript
Recommended Model
Use Whisper medium or large model.
Reasons:
Best multilingual accuracy
Handles code-switching (Hindi + English)
Good noise tolerance
Configuration example:
model = whisper.load_model("medium")
task="translate"
language=None
3. Speaker Identification
Goal
Identify who is speaking in the meeting.
Example transcript:
John: We need to finish the deployment today.
Alice: I will handle the backend changes.
Rahul: I will update the documentation.
Methods for Speaker Detection
Method 1 — Participant Name Mapping (Simple)
Use Google Meet participant list.
When audio energy increases, assign speaker from active participant.
Accuracy: Medium
Method 2 — Speaker Diarization (Advanced)
Use speaker diarization model.
Recommended tools:
pyannote.audio
WhisperX
Output example:
Speaker_1: Hello everyone
Speaker_2: Let's start the meeting
Speaker_3: We need to review the roadmap
Then map speakers to participant names.

6. Hindi + English Mixed Audio Support
Your requirement:
audio will be hindi and english mix
This is called code-switching speech recognition.
Example speech:
Aaj meeting mein we will discuss the deployment plan.
Solution
Use Whisper multilingual model.
Recommended configuration:
transcribe(
  task="translate",
  language=None
)
This:
auto-detects language
converts Hindi → English
keeps English words
Example output:
Today in the meeting we will discuss the deployment plan.
